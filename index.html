About Us

We are a non-profit organization that are partnered with hospitals aroud the world to help people whose illnesses are deemed terminal to get their final wish. We are not a rip-off of any other charity, we promise. Don't look it up.

Why Do We Do This?

We feel bad for those of us who don't get to pick off things from their bucket list because the money they would use to do that is going towards medical expenses. We want to make sure that people who do get their life taken from them preemptively due to terminal illness at least get to leave with no regrets. Thats our mission statement. No one elses.
